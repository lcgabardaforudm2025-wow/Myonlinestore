Main Components of the Top Head Assembly Cylinder Head (Main Casting) The metal body (usually aluminum or cast iron) that bolts to the engine block. Contains ports, valve seats, and combustion chambers. Valves Intake valves let the air-fuel mixture in. Exhaust valves let burnt gases out. Valve Springs and Retainers Keep the valves closed and return them to position after opening. Camshaft (or Camshafts) Operates the valves using lobes that push on lifters or rocker arms. Rocker Arms or Lifters Transfer motion from the camshaft to the valves (varies by design). Head Gasket A crucial seal between the cylinder head and the block, keeping combustion pressure, oil, and coolant separate.
